Book a Skin Treatment
01

Microneedling

A precision skin treatment that creates controlled micro-channels on the surface of the skin.

What it is for

Great for clients wanting a more refined-looking texture, improved glow, smoother-looking skin and a refreshed complexion.

Why it is beneficial

It encourages the skin’s natural renewal process and supports a firmer, more even-looking finish over a course of treatments.

02

Dermaplaning

A gentle exfoliating treatment that removes surface build-up and fine vellus hair.

What it is for

Ideal for dullness, dry surface texture, peach fuzz and clients who want makeup to apply more smoothly.

Why it is beneficial

It leaves the skin looking instantly smoother and brighter, making it a beautiful event-prep or monthly maintenance treatment.

03

Chemical Peels

A professional exfoliation treatment designed to refresh the look of the skin’s surface.

What it is for

Suitable for clients concerned with uneven-looking tone, congestion, texture, dullness or seasonal skin resets.

Why it is beneficial

Peels can help reveal brighter-looking, smoother-feeling skin when selected according to the client’s skin needs and tolerance.

04

Microblading

A brow-enhancing treatment for clients who want a more defined, shaped brow appearance.

What it is for

Helpful for sparse-looking brows, uneven brow shape or clients who want a lower-maintenance brow routine.

Why it is beneficial

It frames the face and creates a more polished everyday look while keeping the finish soft and personalised.
